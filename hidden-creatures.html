Hidden Creatures

Combining thoughtful visual design, user experience, and accessibility to create a cohesive and engaging platform. Custom iconography was developed to address the app's unique focus on niche content like habitats and ecosystems, using thick geometric lines to ensure clarity and consistency. The user interface prioritizes seamless interactions, with features like an easy-to-access search bar and immersive audio functions. Accessibility was a core consideration, with high-contrast color schemes, scalable text, and touch-friendly interfaces ensuring inclusivity.

Overall, Hidden Creatures successfully merges aesthetic appeal with functionality, delivering a distinctive and user-friendly experience.


The visual design of "Hidden Creatures" was carefully crafted to align with the app's unique focus on niche content like 'habitat,' 'trait,' and 'ecosystem.'

Existing icon sets were inadequate, leading to the creation of custom icons with thick, geometric lines to ensure clarity and consistency. These icons complement the app's modern interface, enhancing user experience and encouraging exploration.


The app's user experience was designed to be seamless and intuitive, with an emphasis on functional and aesthetically pleasing interactions. Key features include an easily accessible search bar and immersive audio functions. The design process involved extensive user testing, leading to a visually appealing, user-friendly platform that enhances the exploration of hidden creatures.


Accessibility was a central focus in the development of "Hidden Creatures," ensuring the app is usable by a diverse range of users, including those with visual or auditory impairments. The design incorporates high-contrast color schemes, scalable text, and touch-friendly interfaces. Audio functions provide alternative interactions, broadening accessibility and enhancing the overall user experience.
