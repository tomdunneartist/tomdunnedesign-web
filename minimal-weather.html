Minimal Weather

This concept explores a minimalist approach to weather app design by removing clutter and using a color-based system driven by the outside temperature.

The interface is complemented by clean, bold typography, displaying only essential information such as location, temperature, and date. This minimalism enhances user experience by focusing on what truly matters, offering customization options for necessary data. A future iteration may refine this concept further, simplifying the design even more for a truly streamlined experience.
