Healthy Minds

With this concept health app, I wanted to explore the impact calming design elements would have on the end user.

The use of floating shapes, neutral gradients and flowing illustrations create a sense of relaxation, encouraging the users to engage more deeply with the app. By setting this tranquil tone before any interaction begins, the design promotes comfort and a sustained user engagement, making it particularly effective for health-related applications focused on reducing stress and enhancing well-being.
